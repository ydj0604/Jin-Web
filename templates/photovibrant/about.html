About Me

Hello, my name is Jin and I am currently pursuing MS in Computer Science at UCLA. I am focusing on distributed systems and machine learning during my MS, and those are my main interests. I love solving the challenges that I face when things need to work at big scale. Anything that needs to be optimized or fails because of scalability issues excites me. I also love exploring new technologies, especially the ones related to distributed systems or big data.

From my internship experiences, I came to value code readability and maintainability more than anything else. Meaningful object-oriented class design, clean code, suitable choice of libraries, understandable comments, and even self-explanatory variable/method names are crucial to me when I code. Similarly, I believe simplicity always wins over complexity when designing back-end architectures.
